Maintenance Tips

  • Check tire pressure monthly for optimal fuel efficiency
  • Change engine oil every 5,000-7,500 km
  • Monitor engine temperature to prevent overheating
  • Replace air filters every 20,000 km
  • Test battery voltage regularly, especially in winter
  • Listen for unusual vibrations that may indicate issues
